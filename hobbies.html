Hobbies

As a dog, Oliver, apart from sleeping, enjoys doing the following things:

  • Stealing socks: he carefully takes socks from your bedroom, and drools them in his place.
  • Wagging his tail every time he sees a familiar face.
  • He loves barking at strangers. If the stranger pets him, he will immediately become his friend.
  • Sleeping in your bed as if you had no right to be there.
  • Sitting in your chair once you stand up.
